# Placement and Wage Standards (Policy PLW-200)

## PLW-201 Employment placement definition
A participant is counted as "placed" only if they are employed for at least 30 consecutive
days following the placement date. Placements shorter than 30 days may not be reported as
successful employment outcomes.

## PLW-202 Placement date validity
The recorded placement date must occur on or after the participant's enrollment date.
A placement date preceding enrollment is invalid and must be corrected.

## PLW-203 Minimum wage standard
Reported placements must meet or exceed the applicable federal minimum wage of $7.25 per
hour. Placements below this threshold cannot be counted toward employment outcomes and must
be flagged for review.

## PLW-204 Wage verification
Reported hourly wages must be supported by verification documentation (offer letter, pay
stub, or employer attestation) retained by the grantee.
