# Participant Eligibility (Policy ELG-300)

## ELG-301 Age eligibility
Participants must be at least 18 years of age at the time of enrollment to be eligible for
this grant program.

## ELG-302 Follow-up requirement
Grantees must conduct and document a follow-up contact with each placed participant at the
90-day mark following placement. Missing follow-up documentation is a compliance finding.

## ELG-303 Exit status
Every participant who leaves the program must be assigned an exit status. Permitted values
are: employed, training_completed, withdrew, or exited_other.
