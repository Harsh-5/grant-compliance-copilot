# Grant Reporting Requirements (Policy REP-100)

## REP-101 Quarterly reporting deadline
Grantees must submit the quarterly performance report no later than 45 calendar days
after the end of each reporting quarter. Late submissions require written justification
to the grant officer.

## REP-102 Participant record completeness
Every enrolled participant record must contain, at minimum: a unique participant ID,
enrollment date, program track, and exit status. Records missing any required field are
considered incomplete and must be corrected before the report is certified.

## REP-103 Personally identifiable information
Participant records submitted to the funder must NOT contain personally identifiable
information (PII) such as Social Security Numbers, dates of birth, or home addresses.
Grantees must use the assigned unique participant ID instead.

## REP-104 Data retention
Grantees must retain all source documentation supporting reported outcomes for a minimum
of three years following the close of the grant period.
